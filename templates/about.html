The Challenge

Our BSC Computer Science department faces a critical shortage of physical textbooks. With a growing student population, long wait times for essential study materials have become a barrier to academic success.

Our Solution

Instant Library is a virtual platform designed to bridge the gap between students and knowledge. By digitizing the catalog, we provide seamless, anytime access to high-quality learning resources.

Core Functionalities (MVP)

01

Seamless Access

Register, login, and browse a digital catalog of e-books across core subjects like Mathematics and English.

02

Real-time Alerts

Integrated with AWS SNS to send instant email notifications to both students and staff regarding request updates.

03

Cloud Persistence

A secure request system integrated with Amazon DynamoDB for reliable tracking of book availability.

Social & Business Impact

We ensure equitable access to study materials, fostering a better learning environment and streamlining communication between the student body and library administration.

Security & Architecture

Hosted with professional-grade security, utilizing strict AWS Security Groups (SSH, HTTP, HTTPS) to ensure data integrity and secure public access.
